Review End of Watch

Dennis Johnson August 10, 2023

When looking at movies with an action spin on things they tend to get over exaggerated and blown out of proportion. This is not the case in End of Watch filmed in almost a documentary style feel it can feel like the real thing going on. There is plenty of high intense situations going on as well as the sit and wait aspect of being a police officer. This movie shows raw emotion that many police officers deal with on a daily basis becoming invested and emotional with their job.

The characters in this movie really bring it home for me. The main characters portrayed by Jake Gyllenhaal and Michael Pena clearly have chemistry with each other. This makes the movie feel much more dynamic when they are joking around or talking on a serious topic. This movie will have you sitting on the edge of your seat during the action, laughing while they are joking around, and deeply sad in certain scenes.
